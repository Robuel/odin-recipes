My Childhood Favorite Recipes

These are my favorite recipes and they mostly consist of dishes that my dad and grandma used to make. Most, if not all, of these recipes consist of pork and are generally considered to be quite unhealthy, Nevertheless, these dishes are what I ate as I grew up with so they hold a special very place to my heart. Also, just a warning, but most of them are stews and soup dishes as that's what we cooked most of the time in our home. It probably has something to do with how stews and soups are great with rice or something.

Recipes List

  • Pork and Red Kidney Beans Stew
  • Nilagang Baka or Baboy (Beef or Pork Vegetable Soup)
  • Beef Pares
  • Budget White Sauce and Pasta
